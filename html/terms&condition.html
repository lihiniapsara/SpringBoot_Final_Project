Terms & Conditions

I will donate blood

Only if I satisfy all of the following conditions:

  • I am between the age group of 18-60 years.
  • My weight is 45 kg or more.
  • My hemoglobin is 12.5 gm% minimum.
  • My last blood donation was 3 or more months earlier.
  • I am healthy and have not suffered from malaria, typhoid, or other transmissible diseases in the recent past.
  • My pulse rate must be between 50 to 100 mm without irregularities.
  • My BP Diastolic is 50 to 100 mm Hg and Systolic is 100 to 180 mm Hg.
  • My body temperature is normal and should not exceed 37.5Â°C.

I will NOT donate blood

If I have any of the following conditions:

  • Cold/fever in the past 1 week.
  • Under treatment with antibiotics or other medication.
  • Cardiac problems, hypertension, epilepsy, diabetes (on insulin therapy), cancer history, kidney or liver disease, etc.
  • Major surgery in the last 6 months.
  • Vaccination in the last 24 hours.
  • Had a miscarriage in the last 6 months or have been pregnant/lactating in the last year.
  • Had fainting attacks during last donation.
  • Have regularly received treatment with blood products.
  • Shared a needle to inject drugs/have a history of drug addiction.
  • Had sexual relations with different partners or with a high-risk individual.
  • Tested positive for antibodies to HIV.
  • Consumed alcohol in the last 24 hours.
  • Had dental work in the last 24 hours or a major dental procedure in the last month.
